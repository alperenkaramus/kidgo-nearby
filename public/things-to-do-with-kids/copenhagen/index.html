How to choose a kid-friendly place in Copenhagen

  • Match the stop to child age: stroller/naps, active play, hands-on learning or bigger adventures.
  • Choose one main place and one backup rather than trying to visit too much.
  • Check toilets, shade, food nearby, stroller access and recent map reviews.
  • For travel days, prefer places near transit or your hotel so the route stays flexible.
